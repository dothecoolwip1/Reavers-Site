Red Deer Reavers

A local Buhurt team building strong fighters, strong community, and a safe space to swing steel.

We welcome beginners, parents, gym rats, and anyone who wants something more intense than a regular workout.

Sport snapshot

Full contact fighting in real armor with blunted weapons, supervised by trained marshals.

  • Steel armor with strict standards.
  • Blunted weapons checked for safety.
  • Rounds end when one team is on the ground.

Team

A mix of new and experienced fighters who help each other build skills and confidence.

Community

Family friendly demos, tournaments like Red Deer Rumble, and a supportive crew behind the list.

Highlights

Red Deer Rumble

Travel events

When schedules allow we visit events across Western Canada and beyond.

Community demos

Market days, fairs, and special events with safe, choreographed sets and real hits mixed in.

Practice flow

  • Warm up and movement drills.
  • Strength and conditioning using odd objects and partner work.
  • Soft kit drills and controlled contact.
  • Armor work for people with gear.

Join the team

  1. Reach out on Facebook, Instagram, or Discord.
  2. We will send current practice times and what to bring.
  3. Show up, sign a waiver, and ease into the drills.

You do not have to rush into armor. We want you healthy and having fun first.
