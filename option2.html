About the sport

What is Buhurt

Buhurt is full contact team fighting in medieval style steel armor with blunted weapons. It feels more like rugby in armor than stage fighting.

Fighters wrestle, pin, and strike inside a fenced list. When you are off your feet you are out. The round ends when one team is the only side standing.

  • Steel armor that protects like modern sports gear.
  • Strict safety rules, trained marshals, and event medics.
  • One versus one, small team fights, and big group melees.

Training

How practice works

You can start in normal workout clothes. No armor needed for your first sessions. We build fitness and skills step by step.

Typical week

One strength and cardio focused Reavers Crucible session and one skills practice with soft kit or armor.

You will learn

Footwork, clinching, safe falling, teamwork, and how to move with weight.

No armor yet

That is fine. Come feel it out first. When you are ready we can help plan a realistic armor setup that fits your budget and goals.

Join the team

How to get started

The best way to join is to send us a message and come to a practice. You can watch first or jump into soft kit drills.

  • Message us on Facebook, Instagram, or Discord.
  • Fill out a basic waiver when you arrive.
  • Try some movement and light drills and see how it feels.

Not ready to fight We also need help with coaching, organizing, photos, and list crew.
