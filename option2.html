Red Deer Reavers

A friendly but serious Buhurt team training in the Red Deer area. We mix strength training, soft kit drills, and full steel when you are ready.

Start training What is Buhurt

The sport

Buhurt is full contact armored combat with real steel and real hits. Teams fight inside a fenced list until one side is the last standing.

It is heavy, sweaty, and loud, but built around respect and strict safety rules.

Steel

Fighters wear full steel armor with padded layers.

Team

Work with your line to control space and support each other.

Safe

Weapon checks, rules, and medics keep things under control.

How to start

  • Send us a message on social to say you are interested.
  • Pick a practice date that fits your schedule.
  • Arrive in gym clothes and bring water, no armor required.

First session is about learning movement and seeing if you enjoy the feel of the sport.

Soft kit

We start with padded weapon drills and no metal armor.

Fitness

Strength and cardio that feel like armor without the cost.
