What Buhurt actually feels like

Buhurt is full contact armored fighting with real steel gear and blunted weapons. The goal is to put the other team on the ground using strikes, grappling, and teamwork.

It feels like a mix of rugby, wrestling, and strongman wrapped in armor. There is a big focus on control and respect because everyone has jobs and families to go back to.

  • Steel armor with padding under it, checked for safety.
  • Weapons blunted and inspected before they touch the list.
  • Clear rules, weight classes, and marshals watching each fight.

Who we are looking for

  • Adults and older teens who want something more intense than a normal gym session.
  • People with zero fight background who are ready to learn from scratch.
  • People with combat sports experience who want to try armor and team fights.

You do not need to look like a superhero or already be strong. We can start from wherever you are and build up from there.

How to join a practice

  1. Send us a message on Facebook, Instagram, or Discord.
  2. We send current practice times and what to bring.
  3. You show up in gym clothes with water and a good attitude.

The first night is mostly about moving with the team and seeing how it feels. You do not have to decide anything long term right away.
