About

What is Buhurt

Buhurt is full contact team fighting in real armor with blunted steel weapons. It is closer to rugby than stage combat.

Fighters grapple, pin, and strike inside a fenced list. Once you are off your feet you are out for the round.

  • Full steel armor sets with strong padding underlayers.
  • Strict rules, weight classes, and safety checks.
  • One on one duels and big team melees.

Training

How we train

Come as you are in workout clothes. We build the foundation first, then add armor later.

Fitness

Strength and cardio with odd objects, carries, and pushes that feel like armor.

Skills

Footwork, body control, clinch work, and safe falling so you bounce not break.

No armor yet

That is completely fine. Try soft kit, ask questions, and learn the sport before you spend on gear.
