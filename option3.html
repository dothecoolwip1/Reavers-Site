Armored combat in Red Deer

Red Deer Reavers is a Buhurt team based in the Red Deer area. We train full contact armored combat in a way that is safe, welcoming, and realistic.

What we do

We train for Buhurt, a style of full contact armored fighting with real metal armor and blunted weapons. Fights are in a fenced list and the goal is to put the other team on the ground.

The sport is demanding but very structured. There are weapon checks, armor checks, trained marshals, and clear rules.

Who can join

  • Adults and older teens who want a new challenge.
  • No previous martial arts or sport experience required.
  • Any body type and fitness level. We build strength over time.

What practice looks like

Most beginners start in soft kit and basic workout clothes. We focus on moving safely and building the conditioning you need for armor.

  • Warm up and basic movement drills.
  • Strength and cardio work that feels like fighting in armor.
  • Soft kit drills and controlled contact when you are ready.

How to join

  1. Send us a message on Facebook, Instagram, or Discord.
  2. Pick a practice that fits your work and family schedule.
  3. Show up with water, gym clothes, and an open mind.

Safety and gear

Steel armor is expensive and we do not expect anyone to buy a full kit right away.

  • We help you plan gear in stages so it fits your budget.
  • We follow standard armor and weapon rules used across Canada.
  • We train safe falling, body awareness, and tapping out if something feels wrong.
